Section 1: Navigation

Two fixes that eliminate position loss

Fix 1 — Anchor-preserving zoom

Right now, zooming in/out just changes pixelsPerSecond. The ScrollView doesn't compensate, so whatever you were looking at slides off screen.

✅ Fixed Behavior

clang-A (bigger)
👁 stays centered ✓

How: Before applying zoom, capture the time at the visible center (centerTime = (scrollOffset.x + visibleWidth/2) / pps_old). After zoom, scroll so that centerTime stays at the center (anchor: .center). Zoom logic moves into TimelineView where it has access to scroll state and the proxy.

Fix 2 — Minimap centers the viewport on click

Right now a minimap click scrolls so the clicked time is at the leading edge of the viewport — equivalent to anchor: .leading. The fix is simple: use anchor: .center so clicking the minimap centers your view on that point. Dragging does the same.
